Adding Guard Conditions to Activity Edges

See Also

The UML Modeling feature lets you add  to Activity Edges in an Activity diagram.

To add guard conditions to an Activity Edge:

  1. In the Diagram editor, right-click the target Activity Edge and choose Labels > Show Guard Condition.
    An [unnamed] notation appears on the edge to prompt you for the expression.
  2. Inside the notation, type the expression of the guard condition and press Enter.
