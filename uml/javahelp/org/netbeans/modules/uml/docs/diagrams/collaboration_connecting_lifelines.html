Connecting Lifelines

You can connect two Lifelines via a Connector.

To connect lifelines:

  1. In the Modeling Palette, click the Connector icon .
  2. In the Diagram window, click one of the target Lifelines and drag to the other Lifeline.
    The IDE connects the two lifelines.
  • To re-order Lifelines, simply drag a timeline to a new location.
