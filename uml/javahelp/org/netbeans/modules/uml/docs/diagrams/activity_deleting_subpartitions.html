Deleting Subpartitions

See Also

The UML Modeling feature lets you delete Subpartitions.

To delete a Subpartition:

  • In the Diagram editor, right-click a Subpartition in a Partition and choose one of the following:
    • Partitions > Delete This Partition Column
    • Partitions > Delete This Partition Row
