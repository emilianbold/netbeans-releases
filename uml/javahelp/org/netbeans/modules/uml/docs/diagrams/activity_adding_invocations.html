Adding Invocations

See Also

The UML Modeling feature lets you quickly populate your UML diagrams with Invocations.

To add an Invocation:

  1. In the Modeling Palette, click the Invocation icon .
  2. Click anywhere in the Diagram editor.
    An Invocation is added to the diagram.
  • You can also use the keyboard shortcut Alt-Shift-A to add an Invocation.
