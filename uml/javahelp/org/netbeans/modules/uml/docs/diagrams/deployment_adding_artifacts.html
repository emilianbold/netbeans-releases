Adding Artifacts

You can quickly populate UML diagrams with Artifacts.

To add an artifact:

  1. In the Modeling Palette, click the Node icon .
  2. Click anywhere in the target Diagram window.
    The IDE adds a Node to the diagram.
  3. In the Modeling Palette, click the Artifact icon .
  4. Click anywhere in the target Diagram window.
    The IDE adds an Artifact to the diagram.
  • To add the Artifact to a Node, drop it onto the target Node.
