Using the Navigator Window

See Also

The IDE lets you view an entire diagram in the Overview pane in the Navigator window. This pane displays a scaled-down view of a target diagram, and a scalable, movable box within the pane for focusing, zooming in, and zooming out of specific diagram areas. When you move the box, the target Diagram editor window scrolls the diagram and lets you navigate the diagram freely. When you scale the box, the Diagram window zooms in or out.

To use the Navigator window:

  1. To navigate to a specific part of the diagram, in the Navigator window, move the box to the target area.
    The IDE scrolls through the diagram in the Diagram window, focusing on the target area.
  2. To zoom in on a specific part of the diagram, in the Navigator window, click a handle on the box and decrease the box size.
    The IDE zooms in on the Diagram window area depicted in the Navigator window box.
  3. To zoom out of a specific part of the diagram, in the Navigator window, click a handle on the box and increase the box size.
    The IDE zooms out of the Diagram window area depicted in the Navigator window box.
