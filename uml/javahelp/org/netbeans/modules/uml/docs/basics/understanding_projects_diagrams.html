Understanding Projects and Diagrams

The UML Modeling workflow enforces a hierarchical approach to reducing the complexity in managing large design projects. To use the feature effectively, it is important that you understand the UML Modeling's scope and the relationship between Projects, Diagrams and Diagram elements (objects). A solid understanding of these concepts facilitates collaboration and a superior modeling environment for the enterprise.
