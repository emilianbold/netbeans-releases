Navigation: UML Modeling

See Also

The IDE gives you many ways to navigate UML Modeling functionality, and to navigate within and between UML projects and diagrams. As you work, the feature interacts dynamically with your diagrams and their elements, refreshing itself for every item you open, close, create, modify, or delete.

  • Projects Window. Navigate to all diagrams and elements within UML projects. Associate UML and Java projects. Execute diagram functionality, rename diagrams and elements, and create diagrams from selected elements.
  • UML Design Center. UML Modeling patterns are stored in the Design Center Catalog, from the project level downward, for convenient reuse.
  • Diagram Toolbar. Manipulate the diagrams and navigate among their elements.
  • Pop-Up Menus: Diagram Editor. Find features specific to UML Modeling windows.
  • Keyboard Shortcuts: UML Modeling. Minimize your mouse clicks.
  • Diagram Editor Navigation. Navigate the topography of your diagrams with several different zooming and navigational methods.
