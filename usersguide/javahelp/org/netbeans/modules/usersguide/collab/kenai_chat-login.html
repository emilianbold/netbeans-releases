A Team project can have multiple members collaborating on a project. If the chat room for a Team project is enabled and you are logged in, you can exchange messages in the project's chat room from within the IDE. You can also engage in private chats with members.

Logging in to Chat

To participate in chat sessions you must be logged in to a Team Server and logged in to the chat server. The option to log in for chats is available in the Team Server login dialog. To log in to Chat when you log in to the Team Server, perform the following steps.

  1. Choose Team > Team Server > Login from the main menu.
  2. Enter your username and password in the Login to Team Server dialog box.
  3. Select Online on Chat. Click Login.

Note.

  • If you do not log in for chatting when you log in to the Team Server, you can log in at any time by clicking the Offline icon (  ) in the status bar and enable Online On Chat.

When you are logged in for chatting, the IDE displays the Online icon (  ) and the number of online members in the status bar. The names of the online members are displayed in a tooltip when you mouse over the chat icon in the status bar.

The IDE also displays the Message icon (  ) in the status bar and the total number of project chat rooms with unread chat messages. You can click the number to display a list of the chat rooms with unread messages and open the Chat window.

Logging out of Chat

When you log out of the Team Server you also automatically log out of Chat. If you want to log out of Chat but do not want to log out of the Team Server, perform the following step.

  • Click the the Online icon (  ) in the status bar and disable Online on Chat.
