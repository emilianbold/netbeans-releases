Makefile Wizard: Review Makefile

See Also

The Review Makefile page of the Makefile wizard displays the location and name of the makefile, specifics about the contents of the makefile, and instructions for using it.

  1. Review the information.

    Click Back to go to a previous page if you want to change your selections.

  2. Click Finish when you are ready for the wizard to generate the makefile.
